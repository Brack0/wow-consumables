A bit of role play

There are four types of consumables for raiding (flasks, food, potions and runes. Most of the guild will ask you to have at least flask and food. Runes are not craftable, food is obtainable by everyone (with cooking profession, and affordable at the auction house), flasks and potions require to be alchemist (not everyone can craft them and they are expensive to buy). The app should help people to plan orders to their favorite alchemist and cooker.
